Defect Severity Detection

Our AI system focuses on accurately classifying vehicle defects by severity level

The AutoDiagnose system uses advanced image recognition and machine learning to analyze vehicle defect images and classify them into three severity categories. This helps you prioritize repairs and understand the urgency of maintenance issues.

MINOR

Minor Severity

Issues that don't affect vehicle performance or safety significantly. Can be addressed during routine maintenance with limited urgency.

  • Cosmetic scratches
  • Early signs of wear
  • Non-essential component issues
  • Minor fluid seepage
MODERATE

Moderate Severity

Issues that affect vehicle performance and may eventually impact safety. Should be addressed within the next 500 miles.

  • Significant brake pad wear
  • Minor to moderate fluid leaks
  • Belt wear beyond 50%
  • Suspension component wear
SEVERE

Severe Severity

Dangerous issues that significantly compromise vehicle safety and performance. Vehicle should be repaired immediately or not driven.

  • Brake failure risk
  • Significant structural damage
  • Major fluid leaks
  • Tire sidewall damage/blowout risk

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our vehicle defect detection system

How accurate is the defect detection?

Our AI-powered system has a 94% accuracy rate for common vehicle defects. The system is trained on millions of images and continuously improves with more data.

What types of defects can the system detect?

The system can detect a wide range of mechanical and cosmetic issues including brake problems, engine malfunctions, transmission issues, suspension problems, tire wear, body damage, electrical system failures, and many more.

How are repair costs calculated?

Repair costs are calculated based on your vehicle's make, model, year, and your location. We use real-time data from thousands of repair shops nationwide, combined with parts pricing data to provide you with an accurate estimate range.

How are the three severity levels determined?

Our AI analyzes multiple factors to determine severity level: MINOR (cosmetic or minor maintenance issues with limited impact on safety), MODERATE (issues affecting performance requiring attention within 500 miles), and SEVERE (critical issues that pose immediate safety risks requiring immediate repair).
